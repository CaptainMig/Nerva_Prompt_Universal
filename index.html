How the kit is used

Open the prompt in a Claude Project, ChatGPT, or Gemini chat. Paste a screenshot of a decision you are considering, or describe it. Answer four questions — thesis, evidence, falsifier, why now. NERVA returns a verdict card and a JSON receipt. Paste the receipt into the ledger above. When the decision resolves, mark the outcome. The lift score is whatever the lift score is.

NERVA · Decision integrity layer

A brake with provenance.

NERVA sits between reasoning and action — human or agentic — and returns a structured verdict against five published axes: COMMIT, HOLD, WAIT, CONSULT, TOXIC. Every decision is scored, written to an audit ledger, and reversible by design.

The personal-kit deployment is live and producing real receipts. The architectural extension into agentic gating — a runtime decision layer sitting above operational telemetry — is where the actual market sits.

<USE> How the kit is used
01Open the prompt in a Claude Project, ChatGPT, or Grok chat. Cross-platform verified on Claude; methodology testing underway on Grok.
02Submit a decision — paste a screenshot, paste a document, or describe it in prose. NERVA extracts the structural fields.
03Answer four questions: thesis, evidence, falsifier, why-now. NERVA refuses to score until all four resolve cleanly.
04Receive the verdict card and JSON receipt. Toggle Summary mode for the daily read; Audit mode exposes the full instrument cluster.
05Log the receipt in the browser-local ledger. The receipt is yours; nothing leaves your device unless you choose to share it.
06Mark the outcome when the decision resolves. The lift score is whatever the lift score is. Published openly.
